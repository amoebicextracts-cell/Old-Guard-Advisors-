The Guide

Old Guard Cannabis Advisors is built around one person's unusual path through this industry. Fifteen years of hands-on hash-making and extraction craft before licensure existed, followed by a decade directing cultivation, extraction, and manufacturing for some of the largest vertically integrated operators in Colorado and New York — including 44,000 sq ft of indoor canopy sustaining 15,000+ plants at any given time.

Most consultants know the regulations. Fewer have actually run the wash, walked a canopy at 6am to catch a flip date, or stood in front of a GMP audit defending SOPs they wrote themselves. That combination — artisan judgment plus operational rigor — is what Old Guard brings to every engagement.
